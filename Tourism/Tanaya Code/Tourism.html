Welcome To Amravati

Amravati is the district of Maharashtra state in Central India. Amravati has
many historical places like Gawilgarh fort, Achalpur and also Religious places
like Amba temple, Rukmini Temple, Bhimkund, etc. Not only for
this but also Amravati is recognized for the places like bamboo garden,
Simbora dam, Chikhaldara, and many more.
